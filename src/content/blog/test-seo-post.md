# Understanding Commercial Leasing for Small Businesses

Signing a commercial lease is one of the most significant financial commitments a small business owner can make. It is crucial to have the lease agreement reviewed by a professional to ensure your interests are protected.

## Key Considerations

1. **Rent Reviews**: Understand how rent increases are calculated.
2. **Make Good Clauses**: Know what condition the premises must be returned in.
3. **Term and Options**: Ensure the length of the lease suits your business plan.
