## Areas of Practice

### Dispute Resolution and Litigation
Our practical approach to your legal problems will see cost efficient resolutions beyond the requirement of litigation. But if you still wish to litigate, we will be there all the way to get the best outcome possible.

### Debt Recovery
Have a difficult ledger or loan which you are having trouble recovering, or simply wish to focus on running your business and let us assist in your accounts receivables? We provide streamlined and cost efficient fixed fee debt recovery legal services without any commissions. Transparent and professional processes. You will never need to engage a debt collector again!

### Bankruptcy / Insolvency
What is Insolvency? We provide wide range of insolvency advice and legal services to Trustees in Bankruptcy, Liquidators, Secured and Unsecured Creditors, Bankrupts, Business and Individuals. No matter where you stand whether you are bankrupt or not, the right advice is a phone call away.

### Conveyancing including Complex Conveyancing
Due to our expertise in complex litigation and insolvency, we are often asked to act in complex conveyancing transactions. These can include, bankruptcy sale of real property, family law separation sale of assets, mortgagee sales and other security recovery transactions. We are a one stop shop for all your legal transnational needs from “day to day” purchase and sales, to complex litigated conveyancing transactions. We also deal with Leases too!

### Strata
Whether you are a Strata Manager, Self Managed Strata Scheme or an Owner of a Strata Scheme, we are here to assist in your strata needs from providing practical efficient advices, recovery of strata levies to assisting with General Meeting attendances. Our out of the box solutions to your strata problems comes from years of personal experience on various Strata Committees as well as acting for Owners Corporations in litigation work.
